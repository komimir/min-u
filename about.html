About Min-U

Conquer UPMin on a budget with Min-U, your ultimate guide to hidden gems and student-friendly eats!

Tired of the same old fast food chains and overpriced campus canteens? Min-U is here to revolutionize your UPMin experience! We're your one-stop shop for discovering the tastiest, most affordable eats around campus, from hole-in-the-wall carinderias to hidden mobile stalls and roadside gems.

What sets us apart?

A website for a comprehensive guide of eateries near the UPMin campus is currently non-existent. Most sources that tells about eateries in Mintal are scattered — and they also don’t always include certain important details and information such as store hours, prices, etc. Additionally, they don’t always include lesser known eateries or establishments such as mobile stalls or roadside carinderias.
